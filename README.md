# 📚 Adhyayan | Elevate Your Learning

Adhyayan is a modern, gamified learning ecosystem designed to help students master academic subjects through interactive competition and AI-driven tools. It transforms traditional studying into an engaging experience with real-time battles and personalized progress tracking.

## ✨ Key Features

* **⚔️ Quiz Battles:** Engage in real-time multiplayer quiz competitions with fellow students to test your knowledge.
* **📝 AI-Powered Note Sharing:** Seamlessly share and access study materials enhanced by AI insights.
* **🏆 Global Leaderboards:** Track your ranking and compete for the top spot with a robust streak system.
* **🎯 Lakshya Progress Tracker:** Stay on top of your study milestones and academic goals with integrated tracking.
* **🤖 AI Tutor:** Get instant assistance and clarification on complex topics directly within the platform.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** React, Vite, Tailwind CSS
* **Animations:** Framer Motion (for smooth, gamified transitions)
* **Backend & Auth:** Supabase (PostgreSQL)
* **Architecture:** Modern modular structure optimized for scalability and performance.
